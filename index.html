Short Bio

Dr. Shiqing Wu is currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate, supervised by Prof. Guandong Xu, in the Data Science and Machine Intelligence (DSMI) Lab, School of Computer Science, at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS). He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science, supervised by A/Prof. Quan Bai, from the University of Tasmania in 2022, and a joint B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Auckland University of Technology (New Zealand) and China Jiliang University (China) in 2016.

Shiqing's research interests involve many topics, including:

  • Incentive Allocation Problem
  • Deep Reinforcement Learning
  • Graph Neural Networks
  • Recommender Systems
  • Influence Diffusion Analysis and Influence Maximization Problem
  • Agent-based Modeling and Multi-Agent Systems
